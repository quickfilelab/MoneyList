Frequently Asked Questions

How to restore Premium?

Ensure you are logged into the Google Play Store with the same account you used to make the purchase. Go to the Settings page inside MoneyList and tap "Restore Purchases".

How to backup data?

Navigate to Settings > Backup & Restore. Tap on "Create Backup" and save the generated file to a safe location on your device or upload it to your personal cloud storage.

How to change currency?

Go to the main Settings menu within the app, tap on "Currency & Formatting", and select your preferred default currency symbol.

How to contact support?

You can reach us anytime by emailing support@quickfilelab.com. Please include your app version and device model to help us troubleshoot faster.
