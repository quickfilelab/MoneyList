Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. Data Storage & Privacy

At QuickFileLab, we prioritize your privacy. All your financial data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal financial records, nor do we store them on external servers unless you explicitly choose to export and save a backup.

2. Third-Party Services

To improve app performance and provide specific functionalities, MoneyList uses the following third-party services which may collect anonymized usage data:

  • Firebase Analytics: To understand how the app is used and improve user experience.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: To detect and fix crashes and technical issues.
  • Google Play Billing: To securely process premium feature purchases.
  • Google AdMob: May be integrated in future versions to serve advertisements.

3. Backup & Restore

MoneyList offers a Local Backup & Restore feature. These backup files are created and stored entirely on your local device storage. You are responsible for the security of these files.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@quickfilelab.com
